For your pet's safety and comfort, Bonnie's does not use heated cage drying.

Whenever possible, we file your pet’s nails with a dremel tool instead of clippers for a more accurate, safer trim of your pet’s nails.

Bonnie’s 5 Step Grooming Process

It takes a bit longer, but our process ensures a thorough grooming while being less stressful for your pet.

Haircut & Bath

Just like with humans, a good haircut is all about the details.

  • Rough trim hair and nail trim.
  • Bath with special attention to face and under dog’s tail
  • followed by alcohol-free ear cleaning.
  • Cage rest with a heatless fan to begin the drying process.
  • Brush & fluff dry by shop brusher.
  • Finishing with scissoring trim by groomer.

Bath & Deshed

Even if your dog “just needs a bath”, it also needs grooming.

  • Brush to remove dead hair and nail trim.
  • Bath with shampoo brushed through to remove more dead hair.
  • Special attention to face and under dog’s tail.
  • Cage rest with a heatless fan to continue the drying process
  • Brush & fluff dry by shop brusher.
  • Final brushing and fluff drying.
